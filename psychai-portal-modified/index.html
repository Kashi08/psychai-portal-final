Intelligent, Unified Wellness Care

We utilize technology to support patient reflection and therapeutic outcome mapping.

Zero-Latency Support

Whenever feelings scale, patients get immediate supportive counseling responses from an LLM. Power-matched with Groq's high-speed inference, helping ground patients instantly.

Proactive Safety Warnings

The system constantly flags high-risk patterns or crisis sentiment indicators in logs. Psychologists get instant safety alerts, prioritizing critical interventions.

Intelligent Self-Journals

Structured diary prompts guide clients in writing down thoughts. AI evaluates overall sentiment patterns, mapping wellness trajectories in the client's file.

Automated Prep Digests

Before every session, psychologists review brief, auto-summarized records of self-care entries, capturing progress and points of stress in seconds.

Mindfulness Integration

Clients use custom breathing pacers and grounding timers. Completed exercises populate progress metrics, reinforcing clinical strategies.

Integrated Check-Ins

Real-time encrypted patient-therapist messaging coordinates scheduling, adjustments, and virtual clinic consultations under one roof.

CLINICAL PRIVACY

Secure, HIPAA-Compliant Architecture

Your mental health logs are highly personal, which is why we enforce security protocols. All logs (journals, chat diaries, assessments) are synchronized using Row-Level Security via a secure Supabase cloud network.

End-to-End Encryption

Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, securing client files.

Identity Isolation

Therapist and patient records operate on strictly separated profiles.
